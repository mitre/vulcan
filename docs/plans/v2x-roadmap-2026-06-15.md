## Key Decisions

### v2-btu is bloated (178 sp, 27 cards)
The Phase-0 spike tells you WHICH endpoints the SPA needs. Scope v2-btu AFTER the
spike, not before. Expect to cut it by 50-70%.

### DISA Library Service goes direct to DISA
Discovered 2026-06-15: DISA's Cyber Exchange runs on Salesforce Experience Cloud.
One unauthenticated POST returns the entire STIG/SRG catalog (400 entries). No
Playwright/browser automation needed. ADR: docs/decisions/adr-disa-library-service.md

### SolidQueue for background jobs
Rails 8 native. Runs inside Puma (SOLID_QUEUE_IN_PUMA=true). No Redis, no separate
worker. 24h recurring job for DISA catalog refresh. First background job infrastructure
in Vulcan.

### VersionCurrency is local-first
Green/yellow dots compare against locally imported SRGs/STIGs. DISA catalog service
adds an orange dot for versions available upstream but not yet imported. Air-gapped
deployments see green/yellow only.
